## ISOTMS Overview WIP!


STMS is a centralized school management system designed to integrate academic administration, student tracking, behavioral monitoring, and institutional reporting into a single platform. It supports the daily operational needs of educational staff by combining student records, attendance, performance evaluation, discipline management, and curriculum tracking within one unified interface.

The system manages comprehensive student information, including enrollment data, attendance, academic results, personal traits, oral and written assessments, and long-term academic projections. It also highlights high-performing students through structured recognition mechanisms such as honor rolls and performance summaries.

In addition to academic tracking, STMS includes a behavioral management component that records demerits, detentions, and privilege levels, allowing schools to systematically monitor conduct and apply disciplinary measures. It also supports goal tracking and structured evaluation of student progress over time.

The platform incorporates curriculum-specific tracking, including religious education components such as Bible study progress and page completion monitoring. Administrative tools allow configuration of academic years, subjects, grading conversions, learning centers, user roles, and other structural elements required for institutional management.

Finally, STMS provides statistical and analytical tools that summarize student performance, compare learning centers, and identify academic trends across different time periods. This enables data-driven decision-making at both classroom and institutional levels, ensuring consistent oversight of academic quality and student development.
